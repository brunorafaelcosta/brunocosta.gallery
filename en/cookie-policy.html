What are cookies?

Cookies are small, unique text files that a website sends to your computer when you visit it - these can be session cookies which terminate when you close a browser, or persistent cookies which will remain until you delete them. For example, we use a persistent cookie for your language selection, so that you don't need to select it every time you visit us.

For more details about cookies you can visit www.aboutcookies.org.


How do we use cookies?
