What are cookies?

Cookies are small, unique text files that a website sends to your computer when you visit it - these can be session cookies which terminate when you close a browser, or persistent cookies which will remain until you delete them. For example, a persistent cookie is being used with your language selection, so you don't need to select it each time you visit the site.

For more details about cookies you can visit www.aboutcookies.org.


How are cookies being used?

In addition to the cookie that persists the language selection, the following services are also beeing used which in turn may also use cookies:
